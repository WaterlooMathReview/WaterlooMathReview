The Waterloo Mathematics Review

About


Established in 2011, The Waterloo Mathematics Review is a journal written by students for students. The journal publishes both expository and research articles on topics related to mathematics at the undergraduate level, including computer science, statistics, and mathematical finance. After the hiatus of the past several years, Volume III, issue 1 will be published in August 2017.

Subscriptions

The Review currently does not offer subscriptions. Instead it is distributed free of charge at institutions across Canada. If you would like to see the Review at your school, please contact the editors at editor@mathreview.uwaterloo.ca for more information.
