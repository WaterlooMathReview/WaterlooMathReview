News: Call for Papers

The Review is accepting papers by undergraduates in the Faculty of Mathematics at the University of Waterloo. Papers should be of interest and accessible to the undergraduate population, and may cover any topic within mathematics, original research or not. Details on the submission process are available on the submissions page. Papers must be submitted by January 15, 2011 for consideration for the inaugural issue.
Submission Instructions | March 18, 2017

Waterloo Mathematics Review is Looking for Members.

After a long hiatus, the Waterloo Mathematics Review is looking to rekindle its efforts. The Review is looking for motivated and passionate individuals to carry on the publication. If you are interested in taking part in the Review, please contact the editors at editor@mathreview.uwaterloo.ca.
